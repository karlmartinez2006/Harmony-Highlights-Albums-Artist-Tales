THE WEEKND

Canadian singer, song-writer

ABOUT

×

The Weeknd, born Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer known for his distinctive falsetto voice and genre-blending music. Rising to fame in the early 2010s, The Weeknd has become one of the most influential figures in contemporary R&B and pop music. His introspective lyrics often explore themes of love, heartbreak, and excess, while his atmospheric production style sets him apart as a visionary artist.

FUN FACTS:

  • Mysterious Beginnings: The Weeknd initially kept his identity hidden, releasing his early music anonymously on the internet. This air of mystery helped build intrigue around his persona.
  • Chart-Topping Success: He has achieved multiple chart-topping hits, including "Can't Feel My Face," "Starboy," and "Blinding Lights," the latter becoming one of the longest-running hits in Billboard Hot 100 history.
  • Awards and Accolades: The Weeknd has won numerous awards, including multiple Grammys, MTV Video Music Awards, and Juno Awards, solidifying his status as a music industry powerhouse.
  • Acting Debut: In addition to his music career, The Weeknd has ventured into acting, making his big-screen debut in the acclaimed film "Uncut Gems" alongside Adam Sandler.
  • Philanthropic Efforts: He has been actively involved in charitable endeavors, including donating to various causes and establishing the XO Foundation to support marginalized communities.

LAUFEY

Icelanic-American singer, song-writer

ABOUT

×

Laufey Lín Bing Jónsdóttir is an Icelandic-American musician renowned for her soulful voice and captivating songwriting. Born in Reykjavik, Iceland (23 April 1999), and raised in New York City, Laufey's multicultural background deeply influences her music. She blends elements of jazz, folk, and pop, creating a unique and enchanting sound that resonates with audiences worldwide. Laufey's music is characterized by its storytelling and emotive qualities, drawing listeners in with authenticity and creativity. Her talent and artistic vision make her a rising star in the music industry, with a promising future ahead.

FUN FACTS:

  • Laufey has an identical twin named Junia. Her entertaining TikTok videos of her and her sister gained her a lot of supporters on social media.
  • She achieved prominence in the early 2020s for her success as a jazz-inspired pop artist, with critics noting that her jazz-inflected songs have received considerable success for a genre that has largely declined commercially.
  • Laufey won a GRAMMY at the 66th Annual Awards for Best Traditional Pop Vocal Album for her sophomore album Bewitched.

DANIEL CAESAR

Canadian singer, song-writer

ABOUT

×

Born Ashton Simmonds in Toronto in 1995, Daniel Caesar launched his musical career in 2014, slowly releasing tracks online, ranging in style from acoustic pop fare to soul songs bathed in ambient electronics. Early singles included collaborations with renowned live hip-hop/rock hybridists BadBadNotGood.

FUN FACTS:

  • He is the second eldest of four children to his mother Hollace and father Norwill Simmonds, a pastor and gospel singer who released his first album as a high school student in Jamaica.
  • Caesar attended the Seventh-day Adventist Church and private school in Oshawa. He is of Bajan and Jamaican descent.
  • Daniel Caesar is that before he gained recognition as a Grammy-winning R&B artist, he used to work at a local movie theater in Toronto, Canada

Meriel de Jesus, more commonly known as "mrld, is a Filipina singer and songwriter. Meriel de Jesus, who goes by the mononym Mrld, is a Filipina singer and songwriter. She signed a record deal with Kean Cipriano's label, O/C Records/VViva Records, and released her debut track, "An Art Gallery Could Never Be Unique As You," under that label in 2021.

FUN FACTS:

  • Meriel De Jesus takes pride in her Cebuano heritage, using her exposure to Bisaya pop to craft lyrics that resonate with people’s lives. She signed a record deal with Kean Cipriano's label O/C Records/VViva Records and released her debut track "An Art Gallery Could Never Be Unique As You" under that label in 2021. She gained mainstream popularity with "Art Gallery" as well as the song "Ligaya." In May 2022, she was featured on a billboard ad for Spotify for Women at Times Square.
